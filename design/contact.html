Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your candles burn?

Our premium hand-poured candles typically burn for 40-50 hours depending on usage and environmental factors. Each candle is individually crafted with high-quality soy wax.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. For custom pieces, please contact us directly to discuss your specific situation.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide with carefully calculated rates based on destination. Customs fees, if applicable, are the responsibility of the buyer.

Can I customize my candle?

Absolutely! Our bespoke customization service allows you to choose jar type, wax color, scent profile, and lid option. Contact us for custom orders and bulk inquiries.

What materials do you use?

We use premium soy wax, natural essential oils, and sustainably sourced materials. Our glass containers are recycled where possible, and our packaging is eco-friendly.
